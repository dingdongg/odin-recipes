Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Loved by Koreans and non-Koreans alike, this spicy dish is a staple food where kimchi and rice are fried together with extra ingredients. The result is a delicious dish that's also very healthy!

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Sauce
  • Day-old rice
  • Frozen veggies (thawed and drained)
  • Onions
  • Green onions
  • Green part of green onion for garnish
