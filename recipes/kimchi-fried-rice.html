Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Loved by Koreans and non-Koreans alike, this spicy dish is a staple food where kimchi and rice are fried together with extra ingredients. The result is a delicious dish that's also very healthy!

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Sauce
  • Day-old rice
  • Frozen veggies (thawed and drained)
  • Onions
  • Green onions
  • Green part of green onion for garnish

Steps

  1. Chop kimchi into bite-size pieces.
  2. Saute the kimchi with aromatics to release the fragrance.
  3. Add the rice and toast it thoroughly.
  4. Add the veggies and cook briefly. If you plan to use leftover meat, add it here.
  5. Add the sauce and stir everything together.
  6. Cook until the rice is lightly charred and the veggies are cooked through.
