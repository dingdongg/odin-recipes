Spicy Korean BBQ

Description

Another goated dish. This was an all time favorite time dish of mine growing up. Just try it.

Ingredients

  • Pork (300g)
  • Onion (1)
  • Green onion (1)
  • Minced garlic (2 spoons)
  • Hot chili paste (3 spoons)
  • Hot chili powder (1 big spoon)
  • Pepper
  • Mirim (2 spoons)
  • Soy sauce (4 spoons)
  • Honey (3 spoons)

Steps

  1. Make the spicy seasoning as per the portions above, and let the pork marinate in it.
  2. Heat up a pan and put some cooking oil on. Cook the meat on medium-heat.
  3. Once the meat is cooked, put the veggies in and cook together.
  4. Put in a spoon of sesame oil. Add chopped peppers if you'd like.
